More Than a Job — A Mission

At EE Solutions, our people are our greatest asset. We invest in training, wellbeing, and career growth — because a motivated team protects better.

Paid Training

All new recruits receive fully-paid PSIRA training, first aid certification, and on-the-job mentorship from senior officers.

Career Growth

We promote from within. Whether you start as a patrol officer or technician, there's a clear path to senior and management roles.

Competitive Pay

Market-related salaries reviewed annually, plus performance bonuses, attendance allowances, and overtime pay.

Uniforms & Equipment

All uniforms, protective gear, and work equipment are provided by EE Solutions at no cost to employees.

Employee Wellbeing

Access to an Employee Assistance Programme (EAP), including counselling support and health referrals.

Team Culture

We operate as a family — supporting each other, celebrating success, and maintaining the highest professional standards.
