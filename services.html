FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you install CCTV cameras?

For most residential properties, we can complete the full installation within 1–2 days of your quote being accepted. Larger commercial properties may take longer — we'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do your alarm systems work during loadshedding?

Yes. All our alarm systems include battery backup that keeps your system running for 8–12 hours without power, and our cellular communicators switch to mobile networks when landlines are cut.

Can I view my cameras remotely from overseas?

Absolutely. Our systems connect to a secure mobile app (iOS and Android) that lets you view live and recorded footage from anywhere in the world with an internet connection.
