Built on Community, Driven by Purpose

EE Solutions was founded with one simple belief: every South African — regardless of where they live or what they own — deserves to feel safe at home and at work.

Led by Mr R Tshuma, our team has grown from a small local operation into a nationwide security company trusted by hundreds of homeowners, business owners, and property managers. We understand the unique security challenges facing South Africans, and we've built our services around meeting those needs — intelligently, affordably, and reliably.

Today, EE Solutions operates around the clock, combining trained security personnel with the latest surveillance technology to provide layered protection you can count on.
